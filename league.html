League Rules

  • Team format consisting of 8-12 people; minimum 4 men and 4 women
  • Within each 20 minute game, there is a best 2 of 3 sets to a hardcap of 15 points
  • Within each hour there will be 9 games consisting of: 2 mens doubles, 2 womens doubles, 2 mens singles, 1 womens singles, 2 mixed doubles
  • Each player can only play a maximum of 40 minutes in the hour
  • Format is Round Robin, followed by elimination with an intermediate and an advance bracket
  • League will provide plastic birdies [NOTE: If teams wish to use feather birdies, both teams must agree and teams must supply their own]
  • *If team misses 4 games, they will forfeit 35.00 of their deposit bond
  • **If team misses 9 games, they will forfeit all 70.00 of their depost bond

Elimination Round

Last Updated: January 31, 2017
  • 12 teams will be ranked according to round robin standings, and split into Intermediate and Advanced Pools
  • Each pool will have 6 teams competing in a double elimination format
  • Within each 2 hour match, there will be 9 games consisting of: 2 mens doubles, 2 womens doubles, 2 mens singles, 1 womens singles, 2 mixed doubles
  • Each game will be 30 minutes, best 2 out of 3 sets to 21 points, with a hardcap of 30 points
  • Each player can only play a maximum of 2 games in a match
  • All other league rules apply
